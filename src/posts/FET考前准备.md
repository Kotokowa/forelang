先直接来干货，毕竟你只剩 1 小时了，我尽量讲得短、实用、能立刻用得上。

---

## 一、临考这 1 小时总体建议

* **别再死记硬背新单词**，收益很低，容易更紧张。
* 做 1 小段**英语听力/视频**热身耳朵（2–3 分钟即可）。
* 随便找个话题（比如今天的 Slow moments），**自言自语说 1 分钟**热一下口。
* 手边拿好草稿纸，准备记笔记、列写作提纲用。

---

## 二、听力（Listening）

机考听力最容易丢分在“慌”和“走神”。

**1. 听前 20 秒：一定要做这几件事**

* 快速浏览题目和选项，圈/划出**关键词**：人名、时间、数字、原因、态度等。
* 见到 these words 要提高警惕：

  * 原因：*because, since, as, so that*
  * 转折：*but, however, actually, instead* → 真正答案往往在后半句
  * 总结：*in short, in a word, to sum up, the point is*

**2. 听的时候**

* 简写记笔记，用**符号+缩写**：

  * ppl（people），gov（government），env（environment），↑ increase，↓ decrease，+好 / -坏
* 如果某一句话没听清：**立刻放弃那一句，盯紧下一句**，不要在脑子里倒带。

**3. 听完做题**

* 先用记忆+笔记做一遍，再**必要时回听（如果系统允许）只听相关部分**。
* 不要在一道题纠结太久，**宁可先选一个最可能的**，再看时间回头改。

---

## 三、阅读（Reading）

**1. 顺序与时间**

* 一篇文章：**先看题，再看文**，能省不少时间。
* 检查一下整套题，每篇大概分多少分钟，心里有数。

**2. 做题套路**

1. **先读题干，划关键词**（人物、时间、定位词）。
2. 再快速看文章每段 **首句 + 尾句**，抓大意和结构。
3. 回到有关键词的那一段，**精读 2–3 句** 就能定位答案。

**3. 题型小技巧**

* **细节题**：题目里的关键词在原文附近 1–2 句内。
* **词义猜测题**：看这个词的上下 2–3 句，有没有解释/同义替换/举例。
* **判断题**：注意 not, never, only, all, must 等**绝对词**，经常是命题点。

---

## 四、口语（Speaking）

机考口语往往是对着电脑说，很容易慌。你只要记住一个**“3+1 结构”**：

> **观点句 → 理由句 → 例子句 (+ 简单结尾)**

举个话题的通用框架：

1. **观点句**

   * *Personally, I believe that slow moments are extremely important for students today.*

2. **理由句**（至少 1–2 句）

   * *First, they give us a chance to relax and clear our minds after a long day of study.*
   * *Second, slow moments help us notice the real world instead of staring at our screens all the time.*

3. **例子句**

   * *For example, when I sit in the park without my phone, I often come up with new ideas or simply feel much calmer.*

4. **简短结尾**

   * *So I think we should protect these moments in our daily lives.*

**小技巧：**

* 想不出高级词，就用**简单、准的词**：good / bad / important / helpful / make me relaxed。
* 停顿时可以用：*well, actually, to be honest, let me think…*，不要长时间沉默。
* 语速宁可慢一点、清楚一点，也不要一口气读作文。

## 五、写作（Writing）——快速提分套路

### 1. 时间分配（建议）

* 3–5 分钟：列提纲（关键词，不写完整句）
* 20 分钟：打字写正文
* 3–5 分钟：**专门检查**（拼写、语法、标点、段落）

---

### 2. 通用三段式结构（观点类）

**段落结构：**

1. 引言段：引出话题 + 表明观点
2. 主体段：2–3 个理由（每个理由 1–2 句 + 例子）
3. 结尾段：总结 + 呼吁 / 建议

**可直接套用的句型（记住几句就够）：**

**开头段：**

* *Nowadays, ______ has become a common phenomenon among university students.*
* *There is no doubt that ______ plays an important role in our daily life.*
* *However, people have different opinions about it. In my view, ______.*

**主体段：**

* *First, ______. For example, ______.*
* *Besides, ______. In addition, ______.*
* *What is more, ______, which can greatly improve / influence ______.*

**结尾段：**

* *In conclusion, slow moments are important for students in a fast world.*
* *Only when we learn to slow down can we enjoy our life and study better.*

你可以把 “slow moments in a fast world” 这个主题直接塞进空格里用。

---

### 3. 问题–解决类模板（如果题目是“问题 + 对策”）

**第一段：提出问题**

* *In recent years, more and more students spend too much time on their phones, even late at night.*
* *This trend has brought many problems to their study and health.*

**第二段：说明问题**

* *To begin with, it seriously affects their sleep and concentration.*
* *Furthermore, they may become more anxious and less interested in the real world.*

**第三段：提出对策 + 总结**

* *In my opinion, several measures should be taken to protect slow moments in our daily lives.*
* *First, universities can provide more phone-free spaces such as reading rooms or parks.*
* *Second, students should set a fixed “phone-free hour” every day to relax or talk with friends.*
* *Only in this way can we create more slow moments and find balance in a fast world.*

---

### 4. 快速检查清单（最后 3–5 分钟）

写完后，专门看一遍这些点：

1. **主谓一致**：

   * *students **are***, *smartphone use **affects***。
2. **单复数**：

   * *many student* ❌ → *many students* ✅
3. **时态**：一般论述用一般现在时。
4. **拼写**：

   * 着重看题目里的关键词有没有写错（如 smartphone, environment, university）。
5. **段落**：

   * 至少 3 段，每段开头空行，方便阅卷老师看结构。

---

## 最后 10 分钟，可以这样用：

1. 随便拿一个话题（比如这次 slow moments），**在纸上列一个 3 段提纲**：每段写 3–4 个英文词。
2. 按刚才的模板快速说一遍（当作口语练习 1 分钟）。
3. 再用键盘打 1 段英文（比如开头段），让自己的手指适应打字节奏。

你现在能做的最重要的事：**稳住心态 + 用好这些结构**。
有想临时改的写作句子或想让我帮你套用模板的，可以直接发出来，我帮你快速改一版。

## 一、万能作文句式（按功能分类）

### 1. 引出话题 / 背景

* **Nowadays, … has become increasingly common / popular among university students.**
  现在，……在大学生中变得越来越普遍。

* **We are living in a fast-paced world where …**
  我们生活在一个快节奏的世界，在这里……

* **With the rapid development of technology, more and more students begin to…**
  随着科技的迅速发展，越来越多学生开始……

---

### 2. 表明观点

* **In my opinion, … is of great importance to university students.**
* **Personally, I believe that …**
* **From my perspective, it is essential to…**

（一个观点句 + 后面接 because / for several reasons）

---

### 3. 表原因

* **This is mainly because…**
* **There are several reasons for this. First, … Second, …**
* **One important reason is that…**

例：

* *This is mainly because slow moments help us relax and think clearly.*

---

### 4. 表结果 / 影响

* **As a result, …**
* **Therefore, …**
* **Consequently, …**
* **This can lead to… / This may result in…**

例：

* *As a result, many students find it difficult to focus on their studies.*

---

### 5. 举例

* **For example, …**
* **For instance, …**
* **A good example of this is that…**

例：

* *For instance, some students spend hours scrolling on their phones before going to bed.*

---

### 6. 转折 / 让步

* **However, …**
* **On the one hand, … On the other hand, …**
* **Although…, …**

例：

* *Although smartphones bring us convenience, they also distract us from real life.*

---

### 7. 提建议 / 对策

* **In my opinion, several measures should be taken.**
* **First, students should… Second, they can…**
* **Universities are supposed to…**

例：

* *First, students should set a “phone-free hour” every day. Second, they can spend more time talking face to face with friends.*

---

### 8. 结尾总结 / 呼吁

* **In conclusion, …**
* **To sum up, …**
* **Only when we … can we …**（很好用）

例：

* *In conclusion, slow moments are necessary for students in a fast world.*
* *Only when we learn to slow down can we truly enjoy our life and study.*

## 二、动词“升级”替换（注意根据语境选）

> 括号里是更正式/更书面那种，你挑熟的用就行。

### 1. 常见动作类

* use → **make use of / utilize**
* do → **carry out / conduct** (做任务、研究)
* get → **obtain / acquire / receive**
* put → **place / position**
* help → **assist / enable**
* make (变得) → **make → turn / transform**
* keep → **maintain / preserve**

### 2. 行为 / 改变

* change → **alter / transform**
* improve → **enhance / promote**
* become better → **make progress / make improvement**
* become worse → **deteriorate / get worse**
* stop → **give up / quit / prevent…from…**

### 3. 感受 / 心理类

* like → **be fond of / be keen on**
* love → **be passionate about**
* hate → **dislike / cannot stand**
* make sb. happy → **cheer sb. up / bring sb. joy**
* make sb. worried → **cause concern / make sb. anxious**

### 4. 使用手机 / 媒体（结合你这次考试主题）

* play with phones → **scroll on their phones / check their phones constantly**
* look at phones → **stare at their screens / be glued to their phones**
* waste time → **kill time / spend too much time on…**
* relax → **unwind / wind down**

---

## 三、形容词“升级”替换

### 1. “好/重要”类

* good → **beneficial / valuable / positive**
* very good → **excellent / outstanding / impressive**
* important → **crucial / vital / essential / significant**

### 2. “坏/有害”类

* bad → **harmful / negative / undesirable**
* very bad → **serious / severe**（严重）
* dangerous → **risky / hazardous**

### 3. 形容人/感受

* busy → **occupied / overloaded (with work)**
* tired → **exhausted / worn out**
* relaxed → **calm / peaceful**
* angry → **annoyed / irritated**
* boring → **dull / uninteresting**
* interesting → **attractive / engaging / fascinating**

---

## 四、名词 & 短语“升级”

### 1. 关于时间 / 生活节奏

* slow moments → **quiet moments / peaceful moments / quality time**
* fast world → **fast-paced world / rapidly changing world**
* free time → **spare time / leisure time / downtime**

### 2. 关于学生 / 学习

* school work → **academic work / studies**
* school life → **campus life / university life**
* success → **achievement / accomplishment**
* plan → **schedule / arrangement**

### 3. 关于手机 / 科技

* phone use → **smartphone use / screen time**
* phone addiction → **smartphone addiction / overuse of smartphones**
* social media → **online platforms / social networking sites**

### 4. 常用高级短语（背几个就很加分）

* **play a vital role in…** 在……中起至关重要的作用
* **be addicted to…** 沉迷于……
* **be under great pressure** 处在很大压力之下
* **raise public awareness of…** 提高公众对……的意识
* **find a balance between A and B** 在 A 和 B 之间找到平衡
* **have a negative / positive impact on…** 对……有负面/正面影响

---

## 五、最后小提醒（超临门一脚）

1. 不熟的“高级词”**宁可不用**，用简单但正确的词，比分不地道乱用要高很多。
2. 可以挑 5–8 个你最顺口的高级词 + 3–5 个句型，**现在读两遍，在心里提前安排好：开头用哪个，结尾用哪个**。
3. 写作时先保证：结构清晰（3 段）、语法别太离谱，然后再适当塞一点这些“升级词”，就够了。
